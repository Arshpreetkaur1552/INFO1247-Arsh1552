Isaac Newton

Autobiography

The Sir Isaac Newton was born on 25 December 1642 and died on 20 March 1726/27. He was an English polymath, mathematician, physicist, astronomer, alchemist, and author he was known as the natural philosopher. In 1687, Newton published his great book Philosophic Naturalis Principia Mathematica (Mathematical Principles of Natural Philosophy) divulging a string of earlier findings and forming the base for Classical Mechanics. The pioneer also delved into optics and together with the German mathematician Gottfried Wilhelm Leibniz co-invented infinitesimal calculus, although he was first to develop calculus than Leibniz.
